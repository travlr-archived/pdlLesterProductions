Doc Brooks

“Billy Ray, the Trailer Trash Magician” is not dumb, Brooks explains, not exactly. He’s a little bit different – and a lot “sarcasticly funny”! Billy Ray has a Texas twang, maybe because Doc was himself raised in Texas. If you ask him how Billy Ray decided to pursue magic as a profession, Brooks deadpans.. “it was prompted by a revelation from Billy Ray’s sister” ..who told him she was getting $50 a trick. He takes his comedy to the edge, but the act plays to all in the family. Doc Brooks is an award winning magician.
