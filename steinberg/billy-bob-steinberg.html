Simultaneously intelligent and wacky, Billy Bob Steinberg’s stage show draws on his background in stand-up and musical comedy, theatre, circus arts, and magic.  His spontaneity, unique imagination, and showmanship create a full–speed–ahead act that leaves people laughing uncontrollably.  Using a variety of hysterical props and a banjo — his act is “total off–the–wall, roll–in–the–aisles, spill–your drink fun!" “You’ll laugh so hard your face will hurt!"

In a nutshell...

I was born in New York City,  my family from Brooklyn, with roots in Cripple Creek, Colorado (NO WAY, really?).  I began to perform magic as a youngster, and soon learned to juggle. My defining moment was witnessing my hero, STEVE MARTIN, for the first time!  He was the GREATEST, FUNNIEST, MOST AMAZING PERFORMER I’VE EVER SEEN... Steve performed magic...., I performed magic. Steve juggled...., I juggled.Steve was funny...., I thought I was funny. All I knew, I wanted to be just like him.  So, first things first, sold my prize hog and bought a used banjo (yup, it was pp’d.... previously picked).

In my teens, I was given an opportunity to perform with Clyde Beatty-Cole Brothers Circus, the World’s Largest Tent Circus. It was an incredible experience, and it was SO MUCH FUN!!!

That led to jobs performing for people like Andy Warhol, John Lennon & Mick Jagger, which led to more work with some awesome rock bands such as Deep Purple and Van Halen Hey, I even appeared in Rolling Stone Magazine with Phil Collins!

After graduating college with a degree in music, I began to street perform, and acting in Off Broadway Theater. Soon, I started warming up audiences for TV shows, and opening up for some top headliners. Over the years I’ve appeared with a virtual who’s who of music & comedy, performing in the top theaters & venues throughout North America.

Since then I’ve been “discovered"by Ed McMahon, which led to an appearance with him in Las Vegas, a spot on the 15th Annual American Comedy Awards, and several appearances on the Jerry Lewis Telethon.

Turns out I have a bit of an international appeal, and have headlined at “The Singapore International Comedy Festival", made several appearances on the #1 French Variety TV Show, “Le Plus Grand Cabaret Du Monde”, and extensive touring throughout Canada, New Zealand &Australia, as part of the“World Festival of Magic"

I perform on cruise ships internationally, in casinos (Las Vegas, Reno, Atlantic City, & the Bahamas), corporate events, colleges/universities, conventions, resorts, fairs/festivals, and even amusement parks. Recently I appeared at Busch Gardens Theme Park in Tampa Bay, Florida –180 shows in 7 weeks!!! OUCH!!!

Besides my appearances on the Jerry Lewis Telethon, other TV shows I’ve performed on include “The Statler Brothers Show"(TNN),“Ed McMahon’s Next Big Star"(NBC), “America’s Funniest People”(ABC), “Comedy Tonight”(Fox), “30 Seconds to Fame"(Fox), as well as appearances on MTV, VH-1, CNBC, HBO, &Showtime.

I’m writing &creating new material everyday. My show keeps evolving, it’s truly a work in progress. Even though I’ve been doing this for awhile, I feel like I’m just getting started!
