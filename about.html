Eddy Lester

His interest in show business started at the age of 8, while attending a Coney Island side show. Since that time he’s become a performer.. an owner of a theatrical agency.. a theatrical manager of talent and a promoter/producer of hundreds of shows for many civic organizations and charities.

Among his show business credits are: booking the opening acts and assisting in production for “Evil Knievel at Snake River” and “Evil Knievel in London” with producer Bob Arum.

He was the executive producer for the off-Broadway show and tour of "Occultism in the 70’s.

Mr. Lester booked acts for Mr. Irvin Feld of Ringling Brothers Circus – Amusement Park in Florida. He was instrumental in having Mr. Feld take an interest in Seigfried and Roy having resulted in the Felds becoming their producer until they retired.

As a performer, he was an assistant cruise director aboard the Queen Elizabeth II.. shared the stage with Harry Blackstone Sr.. and participated in summer stock theater. Mr Lester has won awards for his comedy magic act from his peers at magicians' conventions.

For over thirty years, he traveled to casinos around the world co–ordinating training seminars for his client Darwin Ortiz, an author, entertainer, and casino consultant,
