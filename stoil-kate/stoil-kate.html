The Magic of Stoil & Ekaterina

Costume Changing Magic & Comedy Show

This charming European couple is an award winning magic act. They have performed in many countries and appeared on a variety of TV shows, as well as performed for a number of hotels and cruise lines around the world.

If you’re looking for something different at your next event, this dynamic duo can provide just that with their fast–paced, high–energy show complete with music, incredible visual & magic effects and instant costume changes!

Stoil can also use his female assistant and an illusion box to cut her in half and magically put her back together! WOW!!

Regular performers at the Magic Castle in Hollywood, Stoil & Ekaterina will entertain you with their appearing white doves, comedy, unique act and, most important, their original and charming style! If you like magic, slight of hand, comedy and fun, then this is the act for you!
