Juggling To New Heights & Tossing In A Lot Of Comedy

The way Alfonso Guerra sees it, juggling is a noble endeavor. It takes coordination, endurance and skill to pursue what was the entertainment of kings and queens during the Middle Ages. And Alfonso, has become kingly at juggling. He has received awards from the International Jugglers Association. “It’s a sport and an art,“ says Alfonso, “Juggling is endless. Even if I do it for many years, they`ll still be more to learn. ”Alfonso, is in demand for cruise ship work and variety shows. He is a terrific opening act or and act that “does time” with hilarious audience participation while on a unicyle. He juggles bean bags as big as dog house and bowling balls!
